Mysore is one of the most famous tourist destinations in South India. Originally known as Mysuru, it is the largest district of Karnataka. Formerly known as the capital of the ‘Kingdom of Mysore’, Mysore was the name by which the state of Karnataka was popular. The city is praised across the country for its arresting buildings and monuments along with its flamboyant imperial heritage. Its world Heritage-listed palace of the city is perhaps something that brings most travelers here.
